INTRODUCTION

  1. Power factor improvement techniques using FACTS controller
  2. Reactive power Compensation technique is used i.e static var compensator
  3. SVC is used for load compensation of fast changing loads
  4. Among TCR and TSC,TCR elements is used as SVC
  5. FC-TCR method for power factor correction
  6. FC-TCR can be used for power factor correction, flicker reduction, steady-state voltage control, also filter out harmonics from the system

OBJECTIVE

  1. To improve the power factor to enhance the power quality of a line
  2. To optimize the operation of SVC at domestic level.
  3. To maintain the unity power factor of loads mainly inductive.
  4. To know basically operating principle of FC-TCR type SVC.

Algorithm
  1. Measurement of initial reactive power consumed by load
  2. Comparison with reference reactive power
  3. Calculation of suitable firing angle with PI controller
  4. This signal is passed to firing pulse generator
  5. Firing pulse generator produce the necessary current pulse for the thyristor to turn on
  6. The reactor absorbs the reactive Power based on the varying fired thyristor angle

Limitations
  1. Our project only deals with inductive loads
  2. Chances of misfiring of tcr which requires extra tsc circuits and control mechanism would be complex

Conclusion

  • Reactive power consumed by inductive loads was compensated by FCTCR
